About REALTECH CYBERSECURITY CONSULTANTS (K) LTD

REALTECH is a Nairobi-based cybersecurity consultancy providing practical security services to organisations across Kenya and East Africa. We combine local knowledge with international best-practices to deliver measurable risk reduction.

Our Mission

To make strong, affordable cybersecurity accessible to organisations of all sizes — protecting data, systems and people.
